About me

I am now a postdoc at BICMR and I got my Ph.D degree in Fudan University supervised by Prof. Zhiyuan Li and Prof. Meng Chen. My research area is Algebraic Geometry, more precisely I am interested in questions on algebraic cycles and hyper-K\"ahler varieties.
